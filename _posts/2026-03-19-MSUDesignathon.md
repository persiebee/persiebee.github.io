### Marketing & Visual Design  
**MSU Designathon**  

As a member of the marketing team for MSU Designathon, I create visual content and promotional materials to support the event’s branding and outreach. My work focuses on designing posts and graphics that align with the event identity while helping communicate key information to participants. I also contribute feedback on website design to improve clarity and overall user experience.

**Key Contributions:**  
- Design social media posts and promotional graphics aligned with the Designathon brand  
- Create content to support event awareness, registration, and communication  
- Maintain visual consistency across posts and marketing materials  
- Provide feedback on website design to improve usability and clarity  
- Collaborate with team members to refine messaging and visual direction  

**Process:**  
1. **Content Planning:**  
   - Align designs with event goals, timeline, and branding direction  
   - Identify key information to communicate through posts  

2. **Design & Production:**  
   - Create graphics and layouts using design tools (e.g., Figma, Adobe tools)  
   - Iterate on designs based on feedback and evolving needs  

3. **Review & Iteration:**  
   - Share work with the team for feedback and refinement  
   - Contribute input on website design and user experience  

**Collaboration & Skills:**  
- Visual design and branding for digital platforms  
- Social media content creation and communication strategy  
- UX awareness through website feedback and critique  
- Team collaboration and iterative design process  
- Consistency in visual identity and messaging
