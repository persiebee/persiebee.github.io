### Client Website Design & Development  
**West Grand Boulevard Collaborative (WGBC) – ITM 444 Capstone**  

As part of a student team, I work directly with the West Grand Boulevard Collaborative (WGBC), a Detroit-based community development organization, to design and develop a new website that supports their programs, communication, and long-term growth. The goal is to deliver a fully functional, easy-to-manage website along with documentation so their team can independently update and maintain content.

This is an ongoing client project involving regular communication, feedback cycles, and an upcoming in-person conference visit to better understand their work and community impact.

**Key Contributions:**  
- Design and prototype website layouts based on client goals, content, and branding  
- Translate organizational needs into clear site structure, navigation, and page content  
- Develop front-end components using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript  
- Plan for CMS integration (WordPress) to support long-term content management  
- Support creation of documentation so the client can manage and update the site independently  
- Incorporate accessibility considerations, including readable structure and meaningful content organization  

**Process:**  
1. **Client Collaboration:**  
   - Communicate with the client to understand their mission, content, and expectations  
   - Present mockups and gather feedback for approval before development  
   - Prepare for and attend meetings and a live conference to engage directly with stakeholders  

2. **Design & Prototyping:**  
   - Create mockups and style tiles based on WGBC’s branding (color scheme, typography, layout)  
   - Structure key sections such as programs, partners, and community initiatives  
   - Design flexible components that allow for future content updates (e.g., banners, project highlights)  

3. **Development & Handoff:**  
   - Build the website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript  
   - Organize content in a way that supports easy editing and scalability  
   - Develop documentation and guidelines so the client can maintain the site after delivery  

**Collaboration & Skills:**  
- Client communication and professional presentation of work  
- Team coordination and self-managed workflow in a student-led environment  
- UX/UI design, site architecture, and content structuring  
- Front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)  
- Problem-solving and adaptability based on client feedback  
- Focus on usability, accessibility, and long-term maintainability
